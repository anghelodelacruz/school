** Domain

If the following exist the domain will be restricted:

\begin{align*}
\dfrac{a}{0} & &\sqrt{-\#}
\end{align*}

** Intercepts

Find intercepts using the following:

\begin{align*}
\text{y-intercepts} &\rightarrow \text{Let } x=0\\
\text{x-intercepts} &\rightarrow \text{Let } y=0
\end{align*}

** Symmetry

Symmetry exists when:

\begin{align*}
&\text{Symmetry around y-axis } & &\text{Symmetry about origin} \\
&\rightarrow f(-x) = f(x) & &\rightarrow f(-x) = -f(x)
\end{align*}
